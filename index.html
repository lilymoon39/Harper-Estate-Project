About Harper Estate

About the Founder Christian Harper

Christian Harper is the Founder and Managing Director of Harper Estate, a distinguished real estate firm built on the principles of integrity, excellence, and client satisfaction. With extensive experience in the property and housing sector, he has led the company toward sustained growth and innovation within the real estate industry. His strategic vision focuses on delivering high-quality residential and commercial solutions that meet the evolving needs of clients while upholding transparency and professionalism at every stage. Under his leadership, Harper Estate has earned a reputation for reliability, trust, and exceptional service standards.
